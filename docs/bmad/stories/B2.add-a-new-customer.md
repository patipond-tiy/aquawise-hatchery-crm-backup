# Story B2: Add a New Customer

Status: done

## Story

As a Rep,
I want to add a customer who just placed their first order,
so that they enter the pipeline and I can track their cycles.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Modal fields: Farm (TH), Owner, Phone, Zone (province dropdown), interested package (300k / 500k / 1M PL pre-set)
2. Required fields: farm, owner, zone — submission blocked if any are missing
3. On submit: insert `customers` row with `status='active'` and a generated slug; `package_interest` persisted from the "interested package" selection
4. Optimistic update + Thai toast "เพิ่มลูกค้า "{farm}" แล้ว"
5. New customer appears at the top of the customer list without a full page reload

## Dev Notes

### Domain Framing — Critical
"Customers" in Epic B are the **~20–40 nursery operators** (`โรงอนุบาล`) the nursery sells post-larvae (PL) to. They are NOT end farms. Adding a customer means establishing a new nursery-operator relationship, not opening a sales lead. The "interested package" captures PL volume interest as a relationship signal, not a hard commitment. Exact framing is a hypothesis pending validation with P'Bunjong in 2027.
